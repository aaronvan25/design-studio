# /ds:nope — Complete Overhaul

Scrap the current design entirely and start fresh. This re-runs the full design workflow from Step 2 (theme selection) onward.

## Process

1. Confirm with user: "This will replace the entire current design. Proceed?"
2. Read the current page to understand what DIDN'T work (so it's not repeated)
3. Re-run the interactive design selection (theme, layout, animation approach)
4. Explicitly avoid patterns from the scrapped version
5. Full research phase, plan, build
6. Verify and present

## Rules

- The new design MUST feel completely different from the scrapped one
- Different palette, different layout pattern, different animation approach
- Still follows all design philosophy rules — "different" doesn't mean "lower quality"
